Xilun Zhang

I am a Master Student at Carnegie Mellon University SafeAI Lab supervised by Ding Zhao. I work closely with Peide Huang and Mengdi Xu. Previously, I was a Research Assistant at Multi-Agent Robotic System Lab with Prof.Mo Chen. I completed my bachelor's degree with distinction from Simon Fraser University School of Mechatronic Systems Engineering, and completed my Capstone Project on self-driving cars in Autonomous & Intelligent Systems Lab with Prof. Ahmad Rad.

I am passionate about intelligent decision-making algorithms with applications in Robotics. I am particulary interested in few-shot adaptations, skill trasnfer and learning, Robotic Foundation Models.
I am activetly looking for phd oppotunities starting in Fall 2025.
