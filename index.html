Xilun Zhang - 张熙伦

I am a Master Student advised by Peide Huang and supervised by Ding Zhao (SafeAI Lab) at Carnegie Mellon University. Previously, I was a Research Assistant at Multi-Agent Robotic System Lab , under the guidance of Xubo Lyu and Prof.Mo Chen.

I am passionate about intelligent decision-making algorithms with applications in Robotics and Autonomous Driving. I am particulary interested in robot learning and how to make learning algorithms fastly and continously adapt to unseen environments.
